Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Old Bay seasoning
  • Butter
  • Chicken Stock cube

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Pat the chicken breasts dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture. This helps the seasoning adhere better and ensures a nice sear.
  2. Season the Chicken: In a small bowl, mix the Old Bay seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Rub the seasoning mix evenly over both sides of the chicken breasts.
  3. Cook the Chicken: Sear the chicken breasts for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C).
  4. Serve: Serve the chicken breasts with lemon wedges on the side and sprinkle with chopped parsley if desired. Enjoy with your favorite sides!
