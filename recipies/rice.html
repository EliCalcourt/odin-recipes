Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (like canola or peanut oil)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced (any color)
  • 1 large carrot, julienned or thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas or green beans

Instructions

  1. Cook the Rice:
    • Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This removes excess starch and prevents the rice from being sticky.
    • In a medium saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the rice and salt (if using).
    • Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the water is absorbed and the rice is tender.
    • Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
  2. Prepare the Stir-Fry Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and sugar. Set aside.
    • In a separate small bowl, mix cornstarch and water until smooth. Set aside.
